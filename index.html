Our Coaches

Marco Anthony

Founder / Head Coach / Run & Strength

Marco has competed in marathons, ultramarathons and triathlons. He believes having firm goals is key to making consistent progress. His purpose is to help runners define their goals and create a plan to reach them.

Pam Slanders
